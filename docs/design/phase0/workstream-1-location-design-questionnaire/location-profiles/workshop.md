## Premise and guardrail checks

1. Assimilation pressure is subtle, based on handling tools and materials correctly.
2. Player learns expected behavior by observing work rhythms and permission boundaries.
3. Non-stealth reduction can come from helping with tasks and accepted contribution.
4. Knowledge access should tease progression without exposing hidden identity arc.
