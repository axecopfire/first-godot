## Premise and guardrail checks

1. Assimilation pressure is tied to work participation norms and overseer expectations.
2. Player learns acceptable behavior via field routines and task sequencing.
3. Non-stealth reduction comes from honest labor and sponsor endorsement.
4. Keep focus on social fit in local routine, not explicit premise clues.
