## Premise and guardrail checks

1. Assimilation pressure comes from classroom etiquette and role expectations.
2. Player learns through repetition of signs, gestures, and timing.
3. Non-stealth suspicion reduction comes from socially correct participation.
4. Avoid exposition that directly frames player as non-human intelligence.
