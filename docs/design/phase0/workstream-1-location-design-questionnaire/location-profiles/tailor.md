## Premise and guardrail checks

1. Assimilation pressure appears through courtesy and reciprocal behavior, not enforcement.
2. Player learns relationship-driven norms through repeated low-risk exchanges.
3. Non-stealth reduction comes from helping and receiving trust signals.
4. Keep it human-social and avoid explicit metaphysical hints.
