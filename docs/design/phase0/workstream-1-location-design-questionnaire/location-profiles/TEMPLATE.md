## Premise and guardrail checks

1. How does this location reinforce social assimilation pressure in early game?
2. What human-normal behavior is expected and how does the player learn it?
3. What non-stealth path here lowers suspicion by learning or sponsorship?
4. What detail must avoid revealing the hidden player identity too early?
