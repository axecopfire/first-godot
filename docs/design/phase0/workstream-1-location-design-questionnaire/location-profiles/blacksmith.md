## Premise and guardrail checks

1. Assimilation is tested through timing and role alignment with ongoing work.
2. Player learns by syncing to work cadence and offering useful action.
3. Non-stealth reduction comes from accepted labor support.
4. Keep mystery of player nature intact by grounding in social interpretation.
