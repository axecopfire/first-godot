## Premise and guardrail checks

1. Assimilation pressure is strongest as class and authority protocol.
2. Player learns petition and delivery etiquette before deeper access.
3. Non-stealth reduction comes from formal sponsorship and compliance.
4. Keep all reveal pacing constraints intact for hidden identity arc.
