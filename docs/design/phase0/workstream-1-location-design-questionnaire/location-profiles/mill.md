## Premise and guardrail checks

1. Assimilation pressure appears in expectations around work hours.
2. Player learns daily rhythm and when presence is normal.
3. Non-stealth reduction comes from accepted work and respectful timing.
4. No explicit narrative clues about hidden identity should appear here.
